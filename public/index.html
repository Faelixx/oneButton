Instructions:

Desktop/Laptop Users:

Click on any of the character portraits to create a sound specific to the character clicked.


Press keys shown in the character portraits to create a sound specific to the character that shares the key character.

Mobile Users:

Switch your mobile device to loud.


Tap on any of the character portraits to create a sound specific to the character tapped.

NOTE: iOS users will experience gifs not animating correctly.
